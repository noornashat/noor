Welcome to Nissan

Nissan is a multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Japan. It was founded in 1933. Nissan is the world's largest electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer in 2018.

Important Industries

In the last decade, Nissan has made significant strides in the following industries:

  • Electric Vehicles (EVs)
  • Autonomous Driving
  • Connectivity and Infotainment

Nissan GT-R

The Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car that has been in production since 2007. It is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turboV6 engine that produces 565 horsepower and 467 lb-ft of torque.

The GT-R's engine is known for its durability and reliability. It is capable of lasting for over 100,000 miles with proper maintenance.
